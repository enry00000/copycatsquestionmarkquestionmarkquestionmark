"copycats" is an online project opened in november 2025 by enry.


"the purpose of this project is to build an open space where good music and art can be freely shared and spreaded through releases of albums and EPs and through articles and reviews.

copycats is here to make statements. copycats wants to be an echo in the emptiness of the music industry. copycats wants to be a genuine human voice, something that seems to be missing more and more in the music world."
